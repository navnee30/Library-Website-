Graphic and Interior Design Web Sites

  • American Institute of Graphic Arts
    Features professional resources and a career guide for those seeking careers in graphic arts.
  • Communication Arts Magazine
    World's largest magazine for graphic designers, art directors, copywriters, photographers, and multimedia designers. Includes job search help.
  • Critique
    Online site of Critique Magazine, a magazine for graphic designers.
  • InformeDesign
    Contains summaries of current research in interior design. Sponsored by the American Society of Interior Designers and created by the University of Minnesota .
  • Interior Design Directory
    Offers a directory of designers, design schools, trends, and a collection of designer portfolios from around the country.
  • Interior Design Jobs
    Features employment information for interior design professionals.
  • International Interior Design Association
    Links to different chapters, discussion forums and events related to the interior design profession.
  • ISDesigNet
    The online version of Interiors and Sources magazine.
